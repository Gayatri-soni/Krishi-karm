WHY CHOOSE US

We will reduce poverty by unleashing Indian farmer abilities to grow their incomes and manage their natural resources sustainably

AGRICULTURE

Agriculture in eastern India needs intense development to realise its potential. We can help farmers push up the quantity, quality and value of what they produce. Working with farmers growing crops, keeping livestock and managing fisheries, we will help ensure farmers make the most of their land. We can and we will share our expertise in growing the most appropriate and profitable crops. We can help farmers gain access to high-quality inputs, improve soil quality, conserve water and prepare for climate shocks..

BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

How can you pay for the construction of a warehouse if you have no money? How can you save if you have no bank account? There is too little cash to keep Indian farming markets moving. We can help farmers and agribusinesses gain access to the finance they need. We can establish local savings associations, and help small and medium-sized businesses apply for loans. This will inject new life into markets, helping markets to grow, which not only boosts farmer profits, but also provides job opportunities for people making the move from being farmers themselves to seeking work in small towns. Funders are often reluctant to lend to high-risk, inexperienced farming businesses with no paperwork or proof they can become viable. We provide those small businesses with training on how to professionalise and develop into respected enterprises.

ENVIRONMENT

Farm India promotes the sustainable management of fields, forests, grazing lands and water resources. We can use our expertise in participatory forest and rangeland management to bring farmers, governments and other stakeholders together to make agreements on using natural resources sustainably, for the benefit of all. We can help develop plans for the holistic management of landscapes, so that gains from rural development in one region are not cancelled out by losses in another.
